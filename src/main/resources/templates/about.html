Our Journey

From Single Screen to Premium Multiplexes

What started as a single-screen theatre in 1995 has grown into a nationwide chain of premium cinemas known for exceptional experiences.

1995

Founded our first theatre in downtown with a single screen and 200 seats.

2005

Expanded to 5 locations and introduced digital projection technology.

2015

Launched premium formats including IMAX and Dolby Atmos in select theatres.

2023

Reached 25 theatres nationwide with state-of-the-art facilities and services.

Premium Experience

Cutting-Edge Technology & Ultimate Comfort

We believe in delivering an unparalleled cinematic experience through the latest technology and thoughtfully designed spaces.

4K Laser Projection

Our theaters feature state-of-the-art 4K laser projection systems that deliver stunning clarity, brightness, and color depth for an immersive viewing experience.

Dolby Atmos Sound

Experience sound that flows all around you with Dolby Atmos technology, creating a powerful and moving audio experience that matches the on-screen action.

Luxury Recliner Seating

Sink into our plush, power-reclining seats designed with ample legroom and individual armrests for the ultimate in comfort during your movie.
